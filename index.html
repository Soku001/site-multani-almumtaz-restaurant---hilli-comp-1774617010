Our Signature Dishes

Nihari Lahori Style

A slow-cooked, rich beef stew, fragrant with aromatic spices, a true taste of Lahore.

Chicken Karahi

Succulent chicken cooked in a traditional wok with fresh tomatoes, ginger, and green chilies, bursting with flavor.

Lamb Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice layered with tender lamb, infused with saffron and traditional spices, a feast for the senses.

Paya Masala

Hearty and gelatinous trotters curry, simmered to perfection, a classic comfort food.
